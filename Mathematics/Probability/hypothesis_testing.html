Introduction

Once we obtained a statistical model (or hypothesis), we want to determine if the hypothesis is plausible or not. In this section, we introduce frequentist statistical inference and hypothesis testing. While Bayesian inference can replace many frequentist techniques and is especially popular in modern machine learning, frequentist methods remain valuable tools that are sometimes simpler, more standardized, and offer complementary insights.

Suppose we have two hypotheses:
  • The Null Hypothesis \(H_0\)
  • An Alternative Hypothesis \(H_1\)
and we need to choose the one that is more likely.
