Privacy Policy for MATH-CS COMPASS

Last updated: April 30, 2025

Introduction

At MATH-CS COMPASS, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information when you use our mobile application or visit our website.

Information We Collect

MATH-CS COMPASS is designed to provide educational content while minimizing data collection. Our data handling practices include:

  • We do not request or store personal information directly
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes on our website
  • We share limited data with third-party service providers only as described in this policy (Google Play for analytics and payment processing, and Google Forms for feedback collection)

Any data collection mentioned in other sections of this policy (such as payment processing information or voluntary form submissions) is handled with strict attention to privacy and data protection.

Usage Data

Standard usage analytics may be collected through Google Play services. This anonymous data helps us understand how users interact with our app and make improvements.

Payment Information

When you make a donation through our app, payment information is processed through Google Play's billing system. We do not directly collect, store, or process your payment details such as credit card numbers.

The following information may be collected during the Support Developer process:

  • Transaction identifiers
  • Purchase amount and currency
  • Date and time of transaction

This information is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Processing your support
  • Providing customer support related to purchases
  • Complying with legal obligations

Google Play acts as the payment processor and their privacy policy applies to the payment transaction. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app donations

Each third-party service may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information about how they handle your data.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide the services you requested and to comply with legal obligations. When we no longer need to use your information, we will remove it from our systems or anonymize it so that it can no longer be associated with you.

Contact Forms

Our website contains a link to a Google Forms survey where you may voluntarily provide information such as your name, comments, and optionally your email address. This information is collected and processed by Google according to their privacy policy.

We use this information for the following purposes:

  • To receive feedback about our service
  • To respond to your inquiries when you provide contact information
  • To improve our educational content and services

Submission of this information is completely voluntary. Google Forms data is subject to Google's privacy policy, which can be viewed at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
